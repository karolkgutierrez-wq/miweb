Methodology

The DIO+ Methodology

DIO+ stands for Diagnosis, Implementation, and Optimization + Autonomy, a methodology designed to transform reactive or disorganized communications into a clear, organized, and sustainable system.

Weeks 1-2 · Kickoff & Assessment
D
Diagnosis
Assessment Phase

Before building anything, I need to understand how the organization currently communicates: what exists, what's missing, what's working, and what's realistic for the team.

Kickoff & Discovery

Alignment on goals, context, and project scope.

Communications Assessment

In-depth review of current communications using a structured diagnostic framework.

Research & Analysis

Website, social media, materials, audiences, processes, and current team capacity.

Key deliverable

Communications Readiness Review

Weeks 2-4 · Strategy & Plan
I
Implementation
Strategy Phase

Once clarity exists, this phase turns the diagnosis into a clear, actionable communications strategy for the next 12 to 18 months.

Strategic Communications Plan

Defines goals, audiences, messages, channels, campaigns, and priorities.

Narrative + Content Strategy

Core narrative, key messages, campaigns, and content guidelines.

Review & Collaboration

Document review, feedback, and adjustments before next phase.

Key deliverable

Strategic Communications Plan

Weeks 5-6 · Systems & Autonomy
O+
Optimization + Autonomy
Systems Phase

Most consultants end after delivering a plan. This is where DIO+ is different. This phase turns strategy into a practical, sustainable system so the organization can move forward on its own.

Implementation Toolkit

Roles, workflows, editorial calendar, templates, and operating guide.

Recommended Materials

Prioritized list of brochures, flyers, newsletters, and website updates.

Technology + Tool Recommendations

Platform recommendations adapted to the team and budget.

Training & Handoff

Team training, system walkthrough, and project close-out.

Key deliverable

Complete Communications System

+
The '+' in DIO+

"The '+' means I don't just deliver a plan. I also build the team's capacity to implement and sustain it after the engagement ends."

A clear, organized, and sustainable communications system, built to last.
